# Sample Engagement: NorthlineCo Customer Support AI Readiness

**Status:** Reference example. Not a real client. All identifiers are fictional.

## Premise

NorthlineCo is a mid-market B2B logistics-services firm (300 employees,
~$80M ARR) considering an AI assistant for their internal support desk
that handles tier-1 questions from field operations. They have engaged
BHIL for an Express SKU readiness diagnostic with the option to convert
to a Full SKU build engagement.

This sample walks through the engagement end-to-end, demonstrating:

- How the readiness diagnostic surfaces the seven dimensions.
- How a 14/28 composite score with strong governance and weak evaluation
  discipline shapes the Sprint Quote.
- How the cadre activates a subset of agents for the diagnostic and a
  larger subset for the follow-on Full engagement.
- How handoff contracts, HITL gates, and the audit chain operate in
  practice.

## Why this profile

The sample profile is chosen to land near the framework's commercial
sweet spot:

- Mid-market — small enough to have a clear sponsor, large enough to
  have real governance needs.
- Internal support — high-frequency, narrow-scope use case where the
  cadre's evaluation discipline pays off.
- Logistics services — non-regulated by sector but customer-facing
  enough that errors have business consequence.
